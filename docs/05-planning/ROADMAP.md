---

## Phase 1: MVP (Months 1-3)

### Goals
- Launch basic ordering platform
- Onboard 50 restaurants
- Acquire 500 active users
- Prove unit economics

### Deliverables
- Customer Android app
- Restaurant dashboard (web)
- Core features: Search, order, pay, track
- Cashback wallet (no withdrawal yet)
- Basic affiliate tracking

### Key Milestones
| Milestone | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Tech stack decided | Week 2 |
| Backend API complete | Month 1 |
| App alpha | Month 2 |
| Beta launch | Month 2.5 |
| MVP launch | Month 3 |

---

## Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-6)

### Goals
- Scale to 500 restaurants
- Reach 10,000 MAU
- Enable cash withdrawals
- Full affiliate program

### Deliverables
- Cash withdrawal feature
- Full affiliate dashboard
- Restaurant promotions (coupons, BOGO)
- Push notification campaigns
- Analytics dashboard

---

## Phase 3: Scale (Months 7+)

### Goals
- iOS app launch
- Geographic expansion
- Advanced features
- Profitability

### Deliverables
- iOS customer app
- Multi-city support
- Restaurant subscription tiers
- Advanced analytics
- Consider other cuisines
