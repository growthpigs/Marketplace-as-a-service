## PURPOSE

This Memorandum of Understanding establishes the framework for a business partnership to develop and operate marketplace platforms, with the initial project being a food delivery application targeting the Turkish restaurant market in France ("TurkEats" - working title).

---

## SCOPE OF COLLABORATION

### 1. Joint Ventures
The parties agree to collaborate on:
- TurkEats (Turkish food delivery marketplace)
- Future marketplace projects as mutually agreed
- Client projects that align with the marketplace-as-a-service model

### 2. Responsibilities

**Party A (Roderic/Badaboost) shall:**
- Lead all technical development using agentic engineering (AI-assisted coding)
- Product management and roadmap decisions
- UI/UX design and user experience
- Marketing strategy, content, and brand positioning
- Maintain code repositories and documentation
- Provide US-based entity for legal/financial operations

**Party B (Daniel) shall:**
- Lead business development and sales
- Manage client and partner relationships
- Negotiate contracts and partnerships
- Handle operations, logistics, and vendor management
- Finance coordination (invoicing, collections, bookkeeping)
- Legal coordination and compliance
- Coordinate with local partners (e.g., Stéphane for Turkish market)

## FINANCIAL TERMS

### Revenue Sharing
- All revenue generated from joint projects shall be split **50/50** between Party A and Party B
- Revenue includes: transaction fees, subscriptions, affiliate commissions, and any other income from the platforms

### Expenses
- Operating costs shall be tracked and deducted before profit distribution
- Major expenses (>$500) require mutual agreement
- Each party covers their own personal expenses (travel, equipment, etc.)

### Payment Structure
- Profit distributions shall occur **monthly**
- Distributions processed through Badaboost LLC
- Party B responsible for their own tax obligations in their jurisdiction

---

## INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### Jointly Developed IP
- All code, designs, and systems developed for joint projects are jointly owned
- Neither party may sell or license joint IP without the other's consent
- Each party may reuse non-branded components for other projects

### Pre-Existing IP
- Each party retains ownership of IP they bring to the partnership
- Party A: Badaboost development systems, workflows, tools
- Party B: Business relationships, sales methodologies

---

## TERM AND TRANSITION

### Initial Term
- This MOU is effective for **6 months** from the effective date
- Purpose: Test the partnership and business model viability

### Formalization
- If successful, parties agree to formalize the partnership
- Options include: Party B becoming a member of Badaboost LLC or forming a new entity
- Terms of formalization to be negotiated in good faith

### Termination
- Either party may terminate with **30 days written notice**
- Upon termination:
  - Active client contracts will be honored
  - Revenue from existing contracts split per this agreement
  - Joint IP remains jointly owned (no further development)
