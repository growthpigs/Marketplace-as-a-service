# TurkEats - Product Vision

**Project:** TurkEats (Marketplace-as-a-Service)
**Created:** 2026-01-12
**Status:** Phase A - Intake

---

## Vision Statement

TurkEats is a specialized food delivery marketplace targeting the Turkish/kebab restaurant market in France. "Uber Eats for kebabs" - connecting 35,000+ Turkish restaurants with customers through a mobile-first platform with built-in viral mechanics.

---

## Why Now? Why Us?

### Market Opportunity
- **35,000** Turkish restaurants in France - no specialized platform exists
- Strong community network = natural viral distribution channel
- Price-sensitive student market = high engagement with cashback/rewards
- Android-dominant user base aligns with target demographic (build iOS first for design, deploy both)

### Our Edge
| Factor | Advantage |
|--------|-----------|
| Specialization | First dedicated Turkish food platform |
| Community Access | Stéphane's Turkish restaurant network |
| Viral Model | Cashback + affiliate creates organic growth |
| Lower CAC | Community word-of-mouth vs paid acquisition |

## Core Value Propositions

### For Customers
1. **Specialized discovery** - Find Turkish food easily
2. **10% cashback** - Real value on every order
3. **Cash withdrawal** - Differentiator from competitors
4. **Group ordering** - Social, convenient for students

### For Restaurants
1. **Lower commission** - 5% vs 15-30% on major platforms
2. **Community reach** - Access to Turkish food lovers
3. **Self-managed promotions** - Control your marketing
4. **Simple onboarding** - No complex contracts
